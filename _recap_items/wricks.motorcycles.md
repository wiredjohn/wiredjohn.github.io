wricks developed [mikeklubnika.com](https://mikeklubnika.com/) and their own website is very cool. Also [wriks.motorcycles](https://www.wriks.motorcycles/) is a crazy domain name. Wasn't sure how to categorise them because they make games, websites and other cool art - so I've gone with "designer". 

They have so much work I haven't checked out yet, but from what I have seen I especially liked [SHOOT YOURSELF WITH A RIFLE](https://www.wriks.motorcycles/projects/SYWAR) and their unreleased game [Legacy of Animal: Hog's Life 2](https://store.steampowered.com/app/2544680/Legacy_of_Animal_Hogs_Life_2/) looks insane.
