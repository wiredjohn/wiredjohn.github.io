Warning: this website doesn't have an SSL and has a cartoon woman in a bikini on the homepage. I've lazily cut her out of the screenshot.

Tecmo were the game studio behind Fatal Frame and others before merging into another company. 

Their original website is still online, the homepage being a nice example of mid-00's Japanese web design. I particularly like that the left side navigation, and even the main page body itself, creep outside the bounds of their parent containers, as if to say they sit on physically different layers.
