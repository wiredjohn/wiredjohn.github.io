Saw this cool Snorlax Pokemon card on eBay. I was drawn (heh) to it because of the hand-drawn artwork. Turns out it was a promotional card for an illustration contest won by Craig Turvey. 

He submitted his drawing into the 2002 contest and ended up winning so his art actually made it on a card.

Pretty neat!

[Johto Times](https://johto.substack.com/p/interview-with-craig-turvey-snorlax-promo-card) did an interview with him.
