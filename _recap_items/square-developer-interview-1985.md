A fun read, [shmuplations.com](https://shmuplations.com/) translated an old interview with the developers at Square back in 1985. Two years before Final Fantasy. 

It features a quote that probably explains their success and why they still dominate the JRPG market today:

<q>We decided from the start to go for a wider appeal. That's why we included beautiful girls and mecha.</q>

It also includes a screen from *Cruise Chaser Blassty*, which I've never heard of and the interface is insanely cool.
