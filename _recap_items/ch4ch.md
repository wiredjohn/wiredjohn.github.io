Found the homepage of Aaron Patrick, a musician/animator/3D software teacher.

What's interesting is the homepage navigation icons. "music" is an image that changes on hover, "3d" and "contact" are 3D animations rendered as GIFs, but the "patreon" link is a canvas that loads a model of a fish that reacts like it's swimming when you move your mouse over it.

I was really surprised to see a canvas used for such a small element of a page when there is already 3D icons doing something similar right next to it. A nice bit of unexpected interactivity. Go check it out.
