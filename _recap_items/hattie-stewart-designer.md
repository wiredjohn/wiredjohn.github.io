Hattie Stewart is an artist and illustrator with a vibrant style. I particularly like their bold typography and eye-catching imagery, which I think they refer to as "Visual Stimulation". 

Something about their collage work feels *evil* to me. Like the same feeling when I know I'm being subconsciously advertised too. Corporate evil. 

Also their website is cool, I like how the sub-navigation in the [Work section](https://hattiestewart.com/pages/sketchbooks) is basically a copy of the main navigation. Web design credit to [Chris Biron](https://biron.io/).

WARNING: some of the magazine clippings in their work border on NSFW, just so you know before clicking.
