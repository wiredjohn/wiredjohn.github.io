Following the (fake) news of bbno$ allegedly being sued by Blizzard because of his website, I found the classic Diable 2 website still hosted on [battle.net](https://classic.battle.net/diablo2exp/).

It's a cool site to explore with a lot of tricks you don't see today. I really like the border images and buttons that replicate the UI from the game. Pages like the [NPC lists](https://classic.battle.net/diablo2exp/npcs/act1.shtml) have animated idle animations of the characters.

My favourite thing is the [class skill trees](https://classic.battle.net/diablo2exp/popup/necromancer-summon.html), which mimic the menu behaviour in game using an [HTML map](https://www.w3schools.com/tags/tag_map.asp).
