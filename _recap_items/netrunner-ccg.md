NetRunner is a 1996 card game by Wizards of the Coast. The premis *sounds* corny, given that you play as either a hacker or a megacorporation in a cyberpunk(yuck) world, but the style is very cool. 

A lot of the artwork is experimental 3D CGI, often done by artists for Magic the Gathering cards, and the flavour text and narrative are reminiscent of the [Illuminati CCG](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Illuminati:_New_World_Order).

Checks a lot of boxes for me.
