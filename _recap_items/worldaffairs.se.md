Found the newest website of musician Yung Lean/Jonatan Leandoer. 

I like it a lot. It's a single page of clear structured content with maximal use of default styling, but the designer has done so with great care to create a clean and responsive page.

You could call this effort that looks effortless, or something like that. This probably falls under brutalism/neo-brutalist design.

Sad Boys, GTB, Drain Gang, YEAR0001 etc. have always had cool and unique websites. I would love to know who's designing them.
