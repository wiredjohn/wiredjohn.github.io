[Armin Ronacher](https://lucumr.pocoo.org/about/) (who I thought I didn't know but actually do because he's the guy that made Flask) wrote an interesting blog post about the advent of AI agents for programming and the consequences of this, particularly from the perspective of a project maintainer. 

Nice to see someone much smarter than me also be reluctant to fully release themselves to the generative code workflow, but do so in a productive, not-luddite-y way. I think I'll keep up to date with his writings.
