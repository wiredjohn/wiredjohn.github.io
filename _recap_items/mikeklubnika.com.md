I know Mike Klubnika because of their awesome game [Buckshot Roulette](https://mikeklubnika.com/games/buckshot_roulette). 

Found their website which is crazy cool and very onbrand for their design style. I especially like the game catalogue explorer on the homepage: you select 3D rotating icons which change the channel on a CRT T.V. to show footage from the game, which then links you to a dedicated page for that project. 

The site was developed by [wricks](https://www.wriks.motorcycles/).
