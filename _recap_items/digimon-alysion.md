An online game for the current [Digimon Card Game](https://world.digimoncard.com/) has been in development since at least last year and I've only just heard about it. 

That's exciting news, but the website is really noteworthy too. 

There's a lot of layered graphics that idle and react to scroll events in a way I haven't seen done before. I also like the opaque layers of accent animations that travel through the background, the triggered animations for section fly-ins and I like that the "Reduce Motion" option is embedded in the top navigation. 

A slick AND functional site that looks great.
