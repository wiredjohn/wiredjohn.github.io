Ita Toys is the Neocities site of a Japanese-Fashion blogger. 

The web design is insane. 

Clearly a lot of influence from Japenese web design with the use of absolutely positioned elements, text content in images, blatant disregard for mobile users as well as the obvious overall anime/chibi aesthetic - but that being said there is tons of originality in this site. 

I love the colours, the decorative divider and border images, the extreme care that's gone in to positioning each element without abiding to a clear structure is really impressive. 

Not something you ever see made these days.
