I played the demo for the game 500 CALIBER CONTRACTZ, a 3D platformer by [Bryce Bucher](https://www.brycebucher.net/).

The UI of this game really appeals to me. I thought the movement would be awful but it's actually a surprisingly polished and fluid platformer. It'll be interesting to see where it goes. 

Go play the [free demo on steam](https://store.steampowered.com/app/2571410/500_CALIBER_CONTRACTZ/).
